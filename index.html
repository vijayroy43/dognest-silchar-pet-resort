Our team of certified trainers and passionate caretakers treat every furry guest like family. With 24/7 supervision, climate-controlled rooms, and customised care plans, your pet's safety and happiness is our absolute priority.

The Man Behind DOGNEST

Meet the Founder

Vijay Roy

Founder & Head Trainer

Joint Secretary & Life Member — Silchar Kennel Club Puppy Socialisation Obedience & Behavior Training Premium Boarding & Care

Vijay Roy is a professional dog trainer, canine behavior specialist, Joint Secretary and Life Member of the Silchar Kennel Club, and lifelong dog lover who has dedicated his life to understanding and nurturing man's best friend. Specializing in early-stage puppy socialization, obedience, and behavior correction, Vijay's training philosophy focuses on building trust, confidence, and positive communication. He believes that a well-socialized puppy grows into a happy, balanced family companion.

Recognizing the need for a premium boarding sanctuary in Assam, Vijay founded DogNest Silchar Pet Resort. Here, he merges his training expertise with a passion for animal welfare, offering a safe, supervised, and cage-free environment where dogs aren't just boarded—they are pampered, loved, and treated like family. Under his careful supervision, every guest enjoys structured play, exercise, and personalized care, ensuring absolute peace of mind for pet parents.
